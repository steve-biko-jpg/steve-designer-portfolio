About Me

A passionate IT student and digital strategist.

My Mission

My name is Steve, an IT student at Mount Kigali University with a passion for creative design and digital strategy. I specialize in helping educational institutions craft compelling brands and digital identities. My skills in branding, digital marketing, and graphic design allow me to provide comprehensive solutions that are both visually stunning and strategically effective.

Why I Do This

My journey began as a teacher, which gave me a unique, firsthand perspective on the challenges schools face in today's digital world. I saw the need for schools to connect with students and parents in more modern and engaging ways. My passion for design and technology grew out of this experience, and now I'm committed to using my skills to solve these very problems, helping educational institutions grow and thrive.
