My Work

Each project is a story of a challenge met with a creative solution.

Branding and Identity

Case Study: The Green Academy Rebrand

Problem: The Green Academy had a dated logo and inconsistent branding that didn't reflect their modern, eco-friendly curriculum. This was leading to low enrollment.

Solution: I developed a new, vibrant logo and a comprehensive brand guide. We introduced a fresh color palette and typography, applied across their website, brochures, and social media. The result was a 30% increase in inquiries and a renewed sense of pride within the school community.

Digital Marketing

Case Study: St. Mark's School Social Media Growth

Problem: St. Mark's School wanted to boost their community engagement and attract prospective parents on social media, but their content lacked a cohesive strategy.

Solution: We created a targeted social media campaign focusing on student success stories and behind-the-scenes school life. I designed engaging graphics and a content calendar, leading to a 50% increase in follower count and a significant jump in post interactions.

Graphic Design

Case Study: Brookwood College Annual Report

Problem: Brookwood College's annual report was text-heavy and unappealing, making it difficult to convey key achievements to donors and stakeholders.

Solution: I transformed the report into a visually stunning, easy-to-read document. By using clean infographics, bold typography, and strategic photo layouts, the report became a powerful tool for showcasing their accomplishments and securing further funding.
