Digital Marketing

Case Study: St. Michael's School Social Media

Problem: St. Michael's School had a stagnant social media presence that failed to engage the community and attract new families.

Solution: I developed a comprehensive social media strategy focused on showcasing authentic, behind-the-scenes school life. I designed engaging graphics and a content calendar, leading to a 50% increase in follower count and a significant jump in post interactions.

Graphic Design

Case Study: Brookwood College Annual Report

Problem: Brookwood College's annual report was text-heavy and unappealing, making it difficult to convey key achievements to donors and stakeholders.

Solution: I transformed the report into a visually stunning, easy-to-read document. By using clean infographics, bold typography, and strategic photo layouts, the report became a powerful tool for showcasing their accomplishments and securing further funding.
